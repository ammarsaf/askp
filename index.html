Hundred Pre-Marital Marriage Questionnaire

Guide your talking stage phase with a crucial questionnaire to deeply understand your partner, before moving forwards in your marriage journey.

The scoring spectrum might be different for everyone. You can define 1 as Strongly Disagree and 5 Strongly Agree.

The impact indicator have options of Tolerable, Non-tolerable, and None. Use this to indicate how important is the question topic for your marriage.

If you choose Tolerable with score 1, it means the topic would not impact your future marriage very much. However, if the question is Non-tolerable with score 1, it means the issue will significantly impact you. Watch this to have a better understanding.

After completing all the questions, you can get the statistical view of the answers and download the file for your reference. Remember to use this as a guidance, not as the decision maker.

After all, you need to listen to yourself and pray for the best. Good luck.
